Favourite Movies

My top three films of all time.

Fight Club

Fantastic movie starring Brad Pitt, Edward Norton and Helena Bonham Carter. Based on the book by Chuck Palahniuk with themes that still resonate in the 2020s.

Miss Congeniality

Favourite chick flick starring Sandra Bullock. Still sharp with a style of humour and dialogue that is atypical for the genre.

Your Name

Started watching this one around once a year. A Freaky Friday themed anime in the romance genre with likeable characters and an excellent twist.
